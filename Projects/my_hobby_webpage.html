The Badass Art of Gardening

Hi there! My name is Roman and today I'll be showing you how to gain superpowers.

Yeah, you heard right.

Superpowers. 🔥

I'm going to show you how to wield ultraviolet radiation, water, and earth to grow the most
luxurious of herbs: basil and mint. Don't have a green thumb? No worries. I got you. Even if
you've never had so much as a house plant, we're going to take you from complete noob to pro.
Or semi-pro, at least. Let's dig in!

Gardening is a superpower?

Yep. It is.

There's something pretty awesome about being able to take three seemingly basic elements and
transforming them into something that you can eat, transforming them into something close to magic.
I first started getting into gardening because I was tired of buying expensive herbs to cook with.
$5.60 for a tiny sprig of thyme I'm only going to use once in a while? Yeahhh, no thanks.
Besides, I like to cook and as any chef worth their salt knows: the fresher the ingredients, the
better the dish! So I decided to start growing my own herb garden.

Where do I even start though?
