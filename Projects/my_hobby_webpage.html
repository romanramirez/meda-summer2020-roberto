Where do I even start though?

Alright, so, this first step is up to you. You get to choose which plant you want to start with.
Will it be mint? The cool, fragrant, shade-lover? Or will it be basil? The sun-loving, equally-as-fragrant

1. Basil

Ok, so, first up is basil. A flowery, fragrant herb that makes almost any dish taste amazing. It's used in everything from pasta to pizza (basically any dish with an Italian taste profile) and it's also super easy to grow.
Below is a plant 'pokedex' to help break things down:

Basil Basics

Basil loves: 👍

  • Warm sunny weather!
    If temperatures frost bring your plant inside.

  • Being too crowded!
    Give your plant adequate space as not enough air can cause mildew to grow on your basil.

  • Ants
    These bastards will eat your basil and leave the leaves looking like swiss cheese.

Basil hates: 👎

  • The cold!
    If temperatures tend to frost where you live, bring your plant inside.

  • Being too crowded!
    Give your plant adequate space as not enough air can cause mildew to grow on your basil.

  • Ants
    These bastards will eat your basil and leave the leaves looking like swiss cheese.
